# Using Proctor

After logging in you will see an overview of all running, completed, and upcoming tests.

![Teacher Dashboard](/Teacher-Dashboard.png)

## Monitor a test

To monitor a test, click on the desired test (it must be live or upcoming) and then click the "Proctoring" button in the bottom right.

![Test Detail](/Test-Detail.png)

From there you can see all screens of the current participants.

![Proctor](/Proctoring.png)

## Creating a test

To create a test, click "Create New Test" in the top right from the start page. Enter the required details and click "Create".

![Creating an Exam](/Create-Exam.png)

## Settings Page

To change the language or display settings, click the gear icon in the top right — this gives you access to the settings. You can also view your user account from there.

![Settings](/Settings.png)
