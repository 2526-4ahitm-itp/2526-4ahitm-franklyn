# 📘 Project Description

Franklyn is a project that aims to assist instructors
by streaming current activities to the teachers screen during tests and exams.
The program also allows the teacher to switch between different views,
enabling monitoring of all students via a
small dashboard that displays every active student screen, or viewing one individual screen
in the detailed dashboard.
Franklyn will also allow the teacher to look at recordings of previous exams and schedule ahead of time allowing for quick and easy monitoring of all student activity.

## For Students

[Install Franklyn Sentinel]({{< ref "students/installation" >}})

There will be an automatic pop-up window as soon as the teacher starts
the test where you will enter your name and you will be connected to the test.

## For Teachers

Just log in to Franklyn Proctor, choose your test
account prefix (e.g. `testD`) and start the test.
You can then see the list of participants
that joined the test with their name.
